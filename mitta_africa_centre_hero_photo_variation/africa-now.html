SUMMIT 001
DEFINING AFRICA NOW

OVERVIEW

Africa Now is a high-impact, one day summit convening a rising generation of African leaders, creatives, innovators, and changemakers who are actively shaping the continent's political, cultural, and economic reality today.

Across Africa, young people are leading governments, building global companies, exporting culture at scale, influencing billions, and redefining Africa's place in the world.

They are not emerging.
They are operating.

Africa Now brings this generation together to define what Africa is now — not as aspiration, but as fact.
